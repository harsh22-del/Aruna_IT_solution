Our story

From a single substation contract to a full-stack technical services group.

Aruna Nexvolt began in 2024 with a single mandate — re-engineer an aging electrical distribution network for a precision manufacturing client. That focus on reliability shaped everything that followed.

Today our practice spans electrical, fire, ELV, IT and security systems across India and the GCC. We remain an engineer-led firm: every brief is owned by a senior practitioner from feasibility through commissioning.

Mission

To engineer and operate the safest, smartest technical systems for industries that cannot afford downtime.

Vision

To become the most trusted integrated engineering partner for critical infrastructure across emerging markets.
