FAQ

Frequently asked.

Do you take on multi-discipline integrated projects?

Yes — most of our work combines electrical, ELV, fire and IT scopes under a single coordinated delivery team.

What geographies do you serve?

India (pan-India) and select GCC countries. We mobilise on-site engineering teams for projects above a defined threshold.

Do you offer AMC & 24/7 support?

Yes. Our NOC provides SLA-backed monitoring, predictive maintenance and on-call emergency response.

Can you take over a project mid-execution?

We frequently rescue stalled installations — starting with a site audit and gap report before mobilising.

What documentation is provided at handover?

As-built drawings, FAT/SAT records, O&M manuals, training videos and a digital asset register.
